A real platform, not just a simple website.

The goal is not to create a static page. OrikLab is being designed as a structured, expandable product that can grow from a clean launch page into a serious platform.

🧰

Tools Layer

Fast, useful and organized utilities that people can return to every day. Clear categories, simple access and scalable structure.

📚

Resources Layer

Curated websites, guides, explainers and collections that turn the platform into a searchable knowledge base.

💬

Community Layer

A future forum and discussion area where people can ask questions, exchange experience and build trust around the project.

What makes it different

Brand-first structure: OrikLab works as a standalone product under the broader OrikOS vision.
Modular growth: start small, expand carefully, and keep full control of the architecture.
Privacy-aware foundation: legal pages and deployment structure are already in place.
International direction: built to support future multilingual growth and broader audiences.

Current milestone

This version is the clean, professional foundation: custom domain, live deployment, legal pages, and first branded interface.

Domain connected: oriklab.com is active on Vercel.
GitHub workflow: updates can be managed professionally through version control.
Legal baseline: Privacy Policy and Impressum are already available.
Next step: turn this into a polished, fully interactive product.
