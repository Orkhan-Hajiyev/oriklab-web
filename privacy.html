Privacy Policy

1. An overview of data protection

The following gives a simple overview of what happens to your personal information when you visit our website. Personal information is any data with which you could be personally identified.

2. Data collection on our website

Who is responsible for the data collection on this website?
The data processed on this website is performed by the website operator (see Impressum).

How do we collect your data?
Some data is collected when you provide it to us (e.g., in a contact form or forum registration). Other data is collected automatically by our IT systems (e.g., IP address, browser type, time of page access).

3. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to receive information about the origin, recipient, and purpose of your stored personal data at any time free of charge. You also have the right to request that it be corrected, blocked, or deleted.

  • Right to information (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

4. Third-party services

If we use tools like Vercel Analytics, Google Fonts, or GitHub integrations, data may be transferred to third-party servers. We strive to minimize data collection to the absolute technical necessity.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. You can configure your browser to inform you about the use of cookies so that you can decide on a case-by-case basis whether to accept or reject them.
